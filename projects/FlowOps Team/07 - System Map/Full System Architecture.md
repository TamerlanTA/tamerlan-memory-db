## Internal FlowOps Automation Layer

> [!tip] We Automate Our Own Acquisition
> FlowOps practices what it preaches — the entire acquisition system is automated:

- **Upwork Radar:** every 30 min → new jobs → fit score → proposal draft → Slack alert
- **LinkedIn Monitor:** daily → pain posts → draft DM → outreach queue
- **Lead Tracking:** all leads → CRM → statuses → follow-up reminders
- **Demo Library:** Loom videos indexed by offer + niche → instant access mid-conversation
