# 📞 Discovery Call Script

#flowops #sales #discovery

> [!abstract] Call Structure
> **Duration:** 20–30 minutes
> **Format:** Video or phone
> **Goal:** Diagnose pain deeply, establish trust, set up a clear proposal
> **Rule:** You talk 20%, they talk 80%

---

## Pre-Call Setup

Before the call:
- [ ] Review company website + LinkedIn
- [ ] Check tech stack (BuiltWith/Wappalyzer)
- [ ] Identify the most likely pain (based on niche + signal)
- [ ] Have relevant demo queued up (from [[Pipeline D — Demo Library]])
- [ ] Have a note doc open to capture answers

---

## Opening (2 min)

> "Hey [Name], great to meet you. I'm [Your Name] — I help [their type of business] build automation systems that eliminate manual work and speed up [lead response / operations / client onboarding].
>
> I've got 25 minutes set aside for this — my plan is to spend most of it understanding what's going on in your business, and if it sounds like something I can actually help with, I'll put together a specific proposal for you today. Does that work?"

---

## Question 1 — Set the Stage (3 min)

> **"Tell me about your business — what do you do, who do you serve, and how does revenue come in?"**

*Listen for: business model, team size, lead flow, client volume*

---

## Question 2 — Find the Pain (5 min)

> **"What's the biggest bottleneck or frustration in your operations right now? The thing that's either costing you time or slowing down growth?"**

*Let them talk. Don't interrupt. Take notes.*
*If they're vague:* "Can you walk me through a specific example of when that happened last week?"

---

## Question 3 — Quantify the Cost (3 min)

> **"Roughly how many hours a week does [the pain they described] take up — either for you or your team?"**

Follow-up:
> **"And if you put a dollar figure on that — what do you think it's costing you per month in wasted time or missed revenue?"**

*This is where you get the ROI number. Let them calculate it, not you.*

---

## Question 4 — Prior Attempts (2 min)

> **"Have you tried to fix this before? What happened?"**

*Listen for: tools they've tried, freelancers they've hired, why it didn't work*
*This reveals what NOT to pitch and what they're skeptical of*

---

## Question 5 — Decision & Team (2 min)

> **"If we put together a solution that solved exactly this — who else would need to be involved in the decision to move forward?"**

*If there's a business partner or CEO above them:*
> "Would it make sense to have [that person] on our next call? I want to make sure we're not wasting anyone's time."

---

## Question 6 — Timeline (2 min)

> **"How urgent is this for you? Is this something you're trying to solve in the next few weeks, or more of a 'when the time is right' situation?"**

*A prospect who says "we needed this yesterday" is hot. A prospect who says "no rush" needs more urgency created.*

---

## Question 7 — Paint the Future (3 min)

> **"If this problem was completely gone 90 days from now — what does that look like for your business? What does it free up?"**

*This gets them emotionally invested in the outcome. Let them describe their own vision of success.*

---

## Transition to Proposal (3 min)

> "Okay — based on everything you've told me, I have a really clear picture of what's going on. I've built almost exactly this for [similar business] and the result was [relevant outcome].
>
> Here's what I'm thinking: I'll put together a specific proposal — not a generic one, but exactly what I'd build for your setup — and send it over today. It'll include what the system does, what you'd get, and what the investment looks like.
>
> If it makes sense, we move forward. If not, no pressure — you'll at least have a clear roadmap.
>
> Sound good?"

---

## If They Want to Discuss Price Now

> "I want to give you a real number, not a ballpark that ends up changing. Let me put the proposal together and you'll have an exact figure with a breakdown. You'll have it in a few hours."

---

## Post-Call Checklist

- [ ] Update CRM with call notes and pain summary
- [ ] Draft proposal within 4 hours
- [ ] Send proposal with personal note referencing something specific they said
- [ ] Schedule follow-up if no reply within 48 hours
