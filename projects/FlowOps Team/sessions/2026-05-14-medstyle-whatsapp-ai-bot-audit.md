- Used `$almaty-business-audit-proposal` to audit `https://medstyle.kz/` for an owner-facing proposal.
- Researched official website, contacts, service pages, specialists, technologies, 2GIS, 103.kz, and Zoon public listings.
- Prepared offer angle: WhatsApp AI assistant for procedure education, lead qualification, booking handoff, reminders, aftercare check-ins, and admin workload reduction.

## Key findings
- Medstyle has strong trust assets: long operating history, multiple specialist/procedure pages, certified equipment positioning, promotions, WhatsApp, callback forms, and mobile app.
- Main proposal angle is not "fix broken funnel"; it is "convert complex procedure interest into qualified WhatsApp conversations and booked consultations faster."
- Public listings show branch/address inconsistency across sources; bot can help confirm correct branch, hours, service interest, and route inquiries.

## Blockers
- No private CRM/WhatsApp response-time data. Any claim about missed leads must be framed as a hypothesis until tested.
- Medical/esthetic context requires strict guardrails: no diagnosis, no treatment promises, escalation to doctor/admin for contraindications and clinical questions.
