## What was done
- Used the FlowOps Opportunity Engine skill to collect a 30-company Almaty prospect batch from public web/search sources.
- Covered mixed niches: dental, aesthetic medicine, childcare, auto service, logistics, accounting/legal, tourism, events, furniture, interior design, marketing, printing, fitness, hospitality, banquet halls, and veterinary.
- Prepared Telegram-ready rows with company name, activity, likely operational pain, concrete FlowOps offer angle, and public phone contact.

## Key findings
- Common visible signals: phone/WhatsApp-heavy intake, contact forms, callback requests, online booking, quote/estimate flows, and appointment-driven operations.
- Strongest offer matches: Speed-to-Lead System for clinics, dental, fitness, hotels, banquet/event businesses; Ops Automation Sprint for logistics, accounting/legal, design, furniture, printing, and agencies.

## Blockers
- No private CRM/ad account access, response-time testing, hidden review scraping, or direct mystery-shopper calls were performed.
- Pain points are operational hypotheses based on public signals and should be enriched before outbound.

## Next steps
- Pick top 5-7 prospects and run mini-audit checks: response path, form/WhatsApp routing, booking friction, missed-call recovery, follow-up visibility.
- Add selected prospects to CRM with source URL, signal, offer angle, phone source, date collected, and opt-out/compliance fields.
