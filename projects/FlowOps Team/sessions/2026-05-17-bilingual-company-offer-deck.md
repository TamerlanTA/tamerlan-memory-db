## Key findings
- The strongest company-level positioning remains: AI-native systems tied to revenue, speed, and operational clarity.
- A bilingual client-facing deck works best when English and Russian are both structurally integrated, not when Russian is added as a late appendix.
- The premium feel is preserved better with restrained FlowOps typography/layout than with visually noisy effects.

## Blockers
- No blocker for the deck itself.
- If this becomes the canonical company presentation, website copy and outbound messaging should be aligned to the same bilingual positioning.
