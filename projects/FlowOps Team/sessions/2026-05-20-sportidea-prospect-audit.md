## What was done
- Audited public website and business signals for SportIdea / SPORTiDEA.KZ (`https://sportidea.kz`) as an Almaty FlowOps prospect.
- Reviewed homepage, catalog, product page, contacts, about, delivery, payment, warranty, career page, sitemap, and public search snippets for 2GIS/Instagram/listings.

## Key findings
- Business is an established Almaty sports retail/e-commerce operation: site says 5,000+ products, broad categories, physical store at Satpaeva 20A, delivery across Kazakhstan/Russia, Kaspi PAY/QR, BNPL, card payments, cash/bank, legal entity IP SOLMIRA.
- Public channels include phone, WhatsApp, Telegram, Viber/chat/MAX, email, Instagram/Facebook, 2GIS, and a callback form. 2GIS snippet shows 4.8 rating, 241 ratings, 125 reviews.
- Website has many conversion surfaces: cart, "buy in one click", callback, "found cheaper", reviews, credit/instalment, wishlist/compare, product availability.
- Sitemap is large: product/category URLs in the tens of thousands, indicating meaningful catalog/content operations.
- Career page lists open roles in Almaty: sales manager, assistant manager, junior specialist, content manager, marketplace manager. This supports a manual sales/content/marketplace workload hypothesis.
- Product page shows visible technical leakage (`Array` with price/product data), and many product pages show zero on-site reviews despite strong external 2GIS reputation.

## Pain hypothesis
- Main pitch should not be generic AI chatbot. Best angle: "Retail Lead & Order Ops Sprint" or "WhatsApp + Marketplace + Website Lead OS" to unify order/callback/one-click/found-cheaper/WhatsApp/marketplace requests into CRM, staff alerts, SLA tracking, follow-up, and source reporting.
- Secondary angle: website conversion cleanup, especially product-card trust, technical artifacts, review capture from post-purchase, and routing of high-intent product inquiries.

## Blockers
- No secret-shopper test was performed; response time, CRM usage, and exact staff handoff are unverified.
- Public review content was not deeply scraped; 2GIS rating/review counts are from search snippet.

## Next steps
- Draft Loom script focused on 3 visible moments: many channels, high-intent product inquiries, and technical friction on product pages.
- If added to CRM, score SportIdea around 84 with medium confidence; offer should be a 7-10 day ops/conversion sprint before any retainer.
