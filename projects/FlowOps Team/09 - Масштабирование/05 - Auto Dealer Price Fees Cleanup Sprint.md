## Unfairgap

Dealer pricing and sales operations often involve many systems:
- inventory feed;
- website listing;
- ads;
- rebates/incentives;
- CRM quote;
- finance/add-on products;
- salesperson follow-up.

When those systems disagree, the dealer may create deceptive-pricing or junk-fee risk, even when the original cause is messy data.

## What FlowOps sells

**Price + Fees Cleanup Sprint**

FlowOps audits the handoff between website inventory, ad copy, quote templates, CRM, and sales follow-up, then builds alerts and checks around inconsistent fields.

## Audit criteria

Check:
- Does advertised price match CRM quote language?
- Are mandatory fees disclosed consistently?
- Are optional add-ons clearly separated?
- Are rebates/discounts conditional?
- Do sales templates include old or conflicting terms?
- Can the dealer prove what a customer was shown?

## Sprint deliverables

- Inventory/ad-feed audit.
- Website price vs CRM quote comparison.
- Add-on disclosure checklist.
- Sales template cleanup.
- Quote evidence archive.
- Alert when price/fee fields conflict.
- Weekly discrepancy report.
- Loom handoff and SOP.

## Risks

- Some dealers may profit from opacity and resist cleanup.
- Auto compliance has legal nuance.
- Need strong proof before scaling outreach.

## First experiment

Target transparent-positioned dealers first:
- "no hidden fees";
- high review volume;
- multi-location operators;
- dealers publishing detailed pricing pages.

Goal:
- 20 audits;
- 3 discovery calls;
- validate whether the buyer cares enough to pay.
