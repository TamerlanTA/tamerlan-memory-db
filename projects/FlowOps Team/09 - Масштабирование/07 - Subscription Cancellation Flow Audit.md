## Unfairgap

Subscription businesses often optimize signup but neglect the cancellation and refund evidence chain:
- unclear renewal disclosure;
- missing consent record;
- support-only cancellation;
- long cancellation paths;
- refund macros inconsistent with policy;
- no cancellation reason dashboard;
- no proof of what the user saw at signup.

## What FlowOps sells

**Cancellation Flow Audit + Cleanup Sprint**

FlowOps maps the subscription lifecycle, improves cancellation/support workflows, and creates evidence around consent, renewal, refund, and cancellation events.

## Audit criteria

Check:
- Is renewal disclosure clear at checkout?
- Is consent captured and stored?
- Can users cancel without support friction?
- Are refund/cancel policies consistent across site and support macros?
- Are cancellation events logged?
- Can the company prove what the customer agreed to?
- Are state-law differences relevant?

## Sprint deliverables

- Checkout disclosure review.
- Consent event logging.
- Cancellation path map.
- Cancellation support macros.
- Refund/cancel dashboard.
- Customer complaint tagging.
- State-law matrix placeholder for counsel.
- Loom handoff and SOP.
