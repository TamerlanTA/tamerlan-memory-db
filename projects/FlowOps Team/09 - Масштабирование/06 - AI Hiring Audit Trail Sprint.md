Best economic buyer: recruiting agency founder, head of talent, HR operations lead.

## Unfairgap

Teams adopt AI hiring tools before they have an audit trail:
- what tool was used;
- what criteria were applied;
- whether candidates were notified;
- who reviewed AI outputs;
- why a candidate was rejected;
- whether the vendor has bias audit evidence;
- what records can be exported later.

## What FlowOps sells

**AI Hiring Audit Trail Sprint**

FlowOps maps the hiring workflow and adds documentation, review checkpoints, and exportable records around AI-assisted candidate screening.

## Audit criteria

Check:
- Is AI used in screening, ranking, summaries, or assessments?
- Are candidates notified?
- Are criteria job-related and documented?
- Is there a human review step?
- Are rejection reasons logged?
- Is vendor/audit information stored?
- Can the team export an evidence pack?

## Sprint deliverables

- AI tool inventory.
- Hiring workflow map.
- Candidate notice templates.
- Human review checkpoint.
- Rejection reason logging.
- Vendor/bias-audit document vault.
- Monthly audit-ready export.
- Hiring ops dashboard.
- Loom handoff and SOP.
