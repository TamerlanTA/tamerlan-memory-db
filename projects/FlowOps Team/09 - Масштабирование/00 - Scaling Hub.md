## Content

This folder tracks expansion offers for FlowOps beyond the original three core offers.

The direction is **Risk Cleanup Sprints**: narrow, evidence-backed implementation projects where a business has a recurring operational hole tied to fines, lawsuits, demand letters, lost leads, missing records, consent problems, or unsafe data/tool flows.

Do not build all offers at once. Treat this as an experimentation backlog. Promote an offer only after one of these signals:
- 1 paid audit;
- 1 paid sprint;
- 5 serious replies;
- 10 discovery calls with repeated same pain.

## Operating rule

Each offer must stay tied to the FlowOps delivery model:

```text
Free or paid audit -> focused cleanup sprint -> optional monitoring / retainer
```

The product is not "compliance". The product is an operational evidence system:
- map the workflow;
- remove obvious broken handoffs;
- automate the recurring task;
- keep proof;
- give the client a clean handoff.

## First test sequence

1. Test healthcare first for 7 days.
2. Prepare three public/demo cards:
   - healthcare intake cleanup;
   - lead consent evidence chain;
   - ADA website defense prep.
3. Use Pipeline C to generate targeted website audits.
4. Record Loom only for strong-fit prospects.
5. After replies, write a follow-up session note with actual response quality.
