# ✉️ Message Templates

#flowops #sales #templates #messages

> Шаблоны — это только основа. Всегда персонализируй под конкретного клиента.

---

## 1. Upwork Proposal

```
Hi [Name],

I noticed you're looking for [specific thing from job post] — and this is exactly the kind of system I build.

Quick read of what you need: [1 sentence summary of their pain in your own words]

Here's how I'd approach this:
1. [Step 1 of the workflow]
2. [Step 2]
3. [Step 3]

I've built something similar for [type of business] — the result was [specific outcome: time saved / leads captured / process automated].

Quick question before I put together a full proposal: [smart qualifying question about their setup/tools/volume]

Happy to record a quick 2-min Loom showing a similar build if that's helpful.

[Your name]
```

**Правила:**
- Первая строчка должна показывать что ты читал job description
- Никаких пустых "I'm an expert in automation" — только конкретика
- Вопрос в конце = больше шансов на ответ

---

## 2. LinkedIn DM (Cold Outreach)

```
Hey [Name],

Saw your post about [specific problem they mentioned].

I build automation systems for exactly this — [1 sentence what you do relevant to their pain].

Quick idea for your situation: [1 concrete idea, e.g. "an n8n workflow that auto-qualifies incoming leads and updates your CRM before a human even sees it"].

Worth sending a 2-min Loom showing how it works?

[Your name]
```

**Правила:**
- Всегда ссылайся на конкретный пост или конкретную боль
- Одна идея, не список возможностей
- Заканчивай вопросом с низким порогом (Loom, не звонок)

---

## 3. Cold Email (Website Audit Outreach)

**Subject:** Quick automation idea for [Company]

```
Hi [Name],

I was looking at [Company]'s website and noticed [specific observation — e.g. "your contact form goes to email manually" or "no automated follow-up after form submission"].

I build automation systems for [niche] businesses, and typically find 2–3 ways to save [5–10 hours/week] or improve lead response time.

I recorded a 2-min video showing what I found and a quick idea for how to fix it:
→ [Loom link or "happy to send it over"]

Worth a look?

[Your name]
[Website/LinkedIn]
```

**Правила:**
- Subject должен быть конкретным (не "automation services")
- Конкретное наблюдение о ИХ бизнесе — это самое важное
- Loom или предложение его отправить — снижает барьер входа

## 4. Follow-Up (нет ответа)

```
Hey [Name],

Just following up on my previous message.

Wanted to quickly add: I noticed [second specific observation about their business].

Still happy to share the 2-min audit — no commitment needed.

[Your name]
```

## 5. Retainer Pitch (после первого спринта)

```
Hey [Name],

Now that [specific workflow] is live and running, wanted to share a thought.

Most of my clients find it useful to stay connected on a monthly basis — 
mainly to catch edge cases as they come up, make small improvements, 
and add new automations as the business grows.

I offer a simple partnership starting at $500/month that covers:
- Monitoring the workflow
- Monthly improvements
- Quick fixes as needed

No long contracts — just a flexible monthly arrangement.

Worth a quick chat about whether it makes sense for your setup?

[Your name]
```

## 6. Discovery Call Follow-Up (после звонка)

**Subject:** Next steps — [Company] automation

```
Hi [Name],

Thanks for the call today. Quick summary of what we discussed:

**Problem:** [1 sentence]
**Proposed solution:** [workflow name + what it does]
**Timeline:** [X days]
**Investment:** $[amount]

**What's included:**
- [Key deliverable 1]
- [Key deliverable 2]
- [Handoff video + documentation]

To move forward, I'd need:
- [Access to tools]
- [Brief answers to 2–3 questions]

Happy to start [specific date]. Let me know if you have any questions.

[Your name]
```
