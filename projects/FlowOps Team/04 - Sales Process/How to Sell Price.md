# 💰 How to Sell Price

#flowops #sales #pricing #roi

> Цена — это не то что мы хотим получить. Цена — это то, сколько стоит проблема клиента.

---

## Главная формула ROI

```
Часы экономии в неделю × Стоимость часа × 52 недели = Годовая ценность

→ Цена разработки = небольшая часть этой ценности
```

---

## Пример расчёта

> Команда тратит 5 часов в неделю на ручной перенос данных.
> Стоимость часа работы сотрудника: $40.

```
5 hours/week × $40/hour × 52 weeks = $10,400/year

Automation Sprint: $1,500–$2,500
→ Окупается за 2–3 месяца
```

**Как сказать клиенту:**
> "The automation would save your team roughly 5 hours a week. At $40 per hour, that's about $10,000 a year in time costs. The build is $1,500–$2,500, which means you're looking at a payback in 2–3 months — and everything after that is pure savings."

---

## Для Speed-to-Lead

> "If faster lead response helps you close even 1 extra client per month, the automation usually pays for itself quickly."

Пример:
```
1 дополнительный клиент × $2,000 средний чек × 12 месяцев = $24,000/год

Стоимость Speed-to-Lead системы: $750–$2,500
→ ROI >1000% в первый год
```

---

## Для AI Chatbot

> "If the chatbot answers leads 24/7 and books even 2–3 extra appointments per month that you would have missed — the system pays for itself."

---

## Как говорить о ежемесячной поддержке

> "The monthly support fee covers monitoring, fixes, and continuous improvements. As your business changes, the automations need to adapt. It's like insurance + continuous improvement in one."

---

## Возражения и ответы

### "Это дорого"
> "Let me break down what $1,500 actually means for your business. Your team spends [X hours] per week on [process]. At [hourly rate], that's [$Y] per month just in labor costs. The automation pays for itself in [Z months] — and then continues saving that every month after."

### "Я могу найти дешевле на Fiverr"
> "You can — and some cheaper options work fine for simple automations. What I build is a reliable system around your specific business, integrated with your existing tools, tested properly, and with a handoff so your team understands it. The difference is in what happens 3 months later when an API changes or your process evolves."

### "Мне нужно подумать"
> "Totally understand. The main question is usually: is the cost of the problem higher than the cost of fixing it? Based on what you described, [specific pain] is costing you [specific amount]. Happy to send a short breakdown if that would help."
