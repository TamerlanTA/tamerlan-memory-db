## Goal
Build the first working FlowOps pipeline for the Speed-to-Lead offer: capture a new lead, qualify it, update the CRM/storage, send an instant response, schedule follow-up, and notify the team.

## Requirements
- Use n8n as the main automation tool unless another tool is explicitly approved.
- Start with a simple working MVP before adding extra logic.
- Input can be webhook/form/test payload for the first version.
- AI step should summarize the lead, identify intent, urgency, and recommended next action.
- Save lead data and AI summary to the chosen CRM/storage.
- Send instant reply draft or message to the lead.
- Add booking/follow-up placeholder logic.
- Notify the team with lead summary and recommended action.
- Document setup, test payload, and how to run/check the workflow.

## Acceptance Criteria
- One end-to-end test lead successfully runs through the full workflow.
- CRM/storage receives the lead with correct fields.
- Team notification includes lead name/contact, pain summary, fit score or priority, and next action.
- Instant reply/follow-up step is present and testable.
- Workflow is exportable as JSON.
- Short Loom or written handoff explains how it works.
