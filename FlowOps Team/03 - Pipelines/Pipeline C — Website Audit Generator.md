# 🔍 Pipeline C — Website Audit Generator

#flowops #pipeline #outbound #audit

---

## Goal

> Turn cold companies into warm leads through a useful audit.

---

## Target Niches

Property management companies · Marketing agencies · E-commerce stores · Real estate agencies · Local service businesses · Clinics · Dental clinics · Coaches / consultants · Recruiting agencies · Law firms · Home service companies

---

## What the System Analyzes

- Website structure
- Contact forms and booking flow
- Lead capture mechanisms
- Support channels
- Visible tech stack
- Reviews and testimonials
- Job postings (signals of ops pain)
- Social profiles
- CRM / ops clues

---

## What to Find

- Where the company might be losing leads
- What the team is doing manually
- Which follow-ups are missing
- Where CRM flow can be improved
- Which process can be automated first

---

## Output Per Company

| Field | |
|-------|-|
| Company | Name |
| Website | URL |
| Likely bottleneck | 1–2 sentences |
| 3 automation opportunities | Specific ideas |
| Estimated time saved | Hours/week |
| Loom script | What to say in the video |
| Cold email | Ready to send |
| LinkedIn message | Ready to send |
| Follow-up message | For non-replies |

---

## Opener Messages

**Soft:**
> "Hey [Name], I took a quick look at [Company] and noticed 2–3 places where automation could probably save your team time or improve lead response. I recorded a short 2-min audit with the ideas. Worth sending it over?"

**Direct:**
> "Hey [Name], quick idea for [Company]: Your current lead flow looks like it could be improved with an instant response + qualification + CRM update system. This usually helps teams respond faster and avoid losing warm leads. Worth sending you a quick breakdown?"
