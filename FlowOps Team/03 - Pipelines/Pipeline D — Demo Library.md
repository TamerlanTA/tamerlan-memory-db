## Format for Each Demo

- **Length:** 60–120 seconds (Loom)
- **Include:** Problem · Workflow · Tools used · Business result · Price range · CTA

## Loom Structure

```
1. "Here's the problem this solves."
2. "Here's the workflow."
3. "Here's what happens automatically."
4. "Here's what the business gets."
5. "If your process looks similar, I can build this around your tools."
```
