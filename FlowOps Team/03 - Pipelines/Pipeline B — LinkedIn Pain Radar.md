# 💼 Pipeline B — LinkedIn Pain Radar

#flowops #pipeline #linkedin #acquisition

---

## Goal

> Find people who are already publicly talking about their problem.
> **Target: 100 personalized outreaches per week.**

---

## Pain Keywords to Monitor

Posts and comments containing:
- "looking for automation"
- "need help with Zapier / n8n / Make"
- "manual process"
- "CRM mess"
- "too many leads" / "missed leads"
- "customer support backlog"
- "we are scaling ops"
- "need AI chatbot"
- "need workflow automation"
- "recommend a Make.com / automation expert"

---

## How the Automation Should Work

1. Collect public posts by pain keywords
2. Determine if there's a real pain signal
3. Find person's profile and company
4. Check business relevance
5. Generate short personalized message
6. Save lead to database
7. Show top leads for manual sending

---

## Message Template

```
Hey [Name], saw your post about [specific problem].

I build automation systems for this exact kind of ops bottleneck — usually around CRM cleanup, lead routing, follow-ups, and internal task creation.

I had a quick idea for your case:
[1 sentence specific idea].

Worth sending you a 2-min Loom with how I'd structure it?
```
