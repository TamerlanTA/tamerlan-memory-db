# 🔄 Pipeline E — Retainer Conversion

#flowops #pipeline #retainer #mrr

---

## Goal

> Every project should lead to monthly support.
> Convert sprints into long-term partnerships.

---

## When to Pitch

**Not at the very start.** Best moment: right after the first sprint when client is happy with results.

---

## The Pitch Script

> "Now that the first workflow is live, the next step is making sure it stays reliable and keeps improving.
>
> Usually with clients, I stay on as an automation partner — monitoring the system, fixing issues, improving prompts/workflows, and building the next automations as new bottlenecks appear.
>
> This usually works best as a monthly support/improvement plan."
