# 🛤️ Sales Process — 7 Steps

#flowops #sales #process

---

## Step 1 — FIND

Lead comes from:
- Upwork → [[Pipeline A — Upwork Radar]]
- LinkedIn → [[Pipeline B — LinkedIn Pain Radar]]
- Website audit → [[Pipeline C — Website Audit Generator]]
- Referral
- Content / inbound form

---

## Step 2 — SCORE

Evaluate:
- ✅ Is there a clear pain?
- ✅ Do they have budget?
- ✅ Can you solve it?
- ✅ Is there urgency?
- ✅ Retainer potential?

---

## Step 3 — PERSONALIZE

Prepare:
- 1 specific pain summary
- 1 automation idea
- 1 relevant demo
- 1 short message

---

## Step 4 — START CONVERSATION

**Don't sell the full project in the first message.**

Goal of first message:
1. Get a reply
2. Get permission to send Loom
3. Get a call booked

---

## Step 5 — AUDIT / LOOM

Show:
- What's currently inefficient
- How it can be automated
- What the result will be
- What the first step is

---

## Step 6 — PAID SPRINT

Sell the first project (Automation Sprint).
See [[Ops Automation Sprint]] or [[Speed-to-Lead System]].

---

## Step 7 — RETAINER

After delivering results, offer monthly partnership.
See [[Pipeline E — Retainer Conversion]].
