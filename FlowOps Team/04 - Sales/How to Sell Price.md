# 💰 How to Sell Price

#flowops #sales #pricing #roi

---

## Core Principle

> Don't sell hours. Sell savings and impact.

---

## The ROI Formula

```
hours saved per week × hourly value × 52 weeks = annual value
```

### Example

> Your team saves **5 hours/week**, that time is worth **$40/hour**:
> 5 × 40 × 52 = **$10,400/year**
>
> So a **$1,500–$2,500 automation setup** is a no-brainer if it removes that work reliably.

---

## For Lead-Heavy Businesses

> "If faster response helps you close even **1 extra client per month**, the automation usually pays for itself quickly."

---

## Anchoring Technique

Always present the annual value BEFORE the price:

1. Calculate time/money lost
2. Show annual impact
3. Then present your price as a fraction of that
