# 📞 Discovery Call Script

#flowops #sales #call

---

## Goal

Understand the pain deeply before proposing a solution.

---

## 7 Key Questions

### 1. Current Process
> "How does this process work today?"

*Understand what they're doing manually and how.*

---

### 2. Pain Point
> "What part takes the most time or causes the most mistakes?"

*Find the biggest friction point.*

---

### 3. Volume
> "How often does this happen per week / month?"

*Quantify the scale of the problem.*

---

### 4. Cost
> "How much time does your team spend on this?"

*Build the ROI argument — see [[How to Sell Price]].*

---

### 5. Impact
> "What happens if this is not handled fast enough?"

*Reveal the cost of inaction — lost leads, errors, unhappy clients.*

---

### 6. Existing Tools
> "What tools are already involved?"

*n8n / Make / Zapier / CRM / email / calendar — understand the tech stack.*

---

### 7. First Automation
> "If we could automate only one part first, which would create the biggest impact?"

*Let them prioritize — increases buy-in.*
