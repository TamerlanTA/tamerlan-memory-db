## The Core Pitch

> We don't just build one automation and disappear.
> We become your **long-term automation partner**: we map your operations, build the highest-ROI workflows first, monitor what breaks, improve the system over time, and keep reducing manual work month by month.

---

## Business Model

```
First project
→ Quick automation sprint
→ Proven result
→ Long-term partnership
```

The goal is NOT just to close a $500–$1,000 project.
The goal is: **enter through a clear pain, deliver fast results, convert to monthly support.**

---

## Why This Works

- Client already trusts you after the sprint
- They've seen results — easy to justify ongoing cost
- You build recurring revenue instead of chasing new projects
- You keep improving the system → more value over time
