# 🪜 Product Ladder

#flowops #strategy #productladder

---

## Step 1 — Free Entry: Automation Audit

**Goal:** Start the conversation.

> "I'll review your current workflow and show 3 automation opportunities that can save your team time or help you respond to leads faster."

**Format options:**
- Short Loom video
- PDF / Notion audit
- Message with 3 specific ideas

---

## Step 2 — First Paid Project: Automation Sprint

**Timeline:** 5–7 days
**Price:** $750 – $2,500

> "In 5–7 days, we automate one high-impact workflow in your business."

**What's included:**
- Process mapping
- Workflow design
- Implementation (n8n / Make / Zapier)
- AI / API integrations
- Testing
- Handoff video
- Basic documentation

**Goal:** Prove results fast.

---

## Step 3 — Ongoing: Long-Term Automation Partner

**Price:** $500 – $2,000+ / month

| Tier | Price | Includes |
|------|-------|----------|
| Starter | $500/mo | Monitoring, small fixes, monthly call, 1 improvement/mo |
| Growth | $1,000/mo | Priority support, 2–3 improvements/mo, reporting, planning |
| Partner | $2,000+/mo | Strategy, new workflows monthly, docs, team training, AI consulting |

**Goal:** Stable monthly recurring revenue.
