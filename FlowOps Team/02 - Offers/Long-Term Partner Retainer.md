# 🔁 Long-Term Partner Retainer

#flowops #offer #retainer #mrr

---

## When to Pitch

**Not at the start aggressively.** Best moment: after the first sprint, when the client has seen results.

---

## The Pitch

> "Now that the first workflow is live, the next step is making sure it stays reliable and keeps improving.
> Usually with clients, I stay on as an automation partner — monitoring the system, fixing issues, improving prompts/workflows, and building the next automations as new bottlenecks appear."

---

## Tiers

### 🟢 Starter — $500/month
- Workflow monitoring
- Small fixes
- Monthly improvement call
- Basic support
- 1 small improvement/month

### 🔵 Growth — $1,000/month
- Everything in Starter
- Priority support
- 2–3 workflow improvements/month
- Reporting
- New automation planning

### 🟣 Partner — $2,000+/month
- Ongoing automation strategy
- New workflows every month
- System monitoring
- Documentation
- Team training
- AI / process consulting
