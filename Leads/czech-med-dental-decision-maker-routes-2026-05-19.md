## Practical Calling Script

Use this when the public number is reception but a named decision maker exists:

```text
Dobrý den, my name is Tamerlan. I’m not calling about a patient appointment.
I prepared a short operational idea for [Clinic] about faster patient inquiry routing and appointment follow-up.
Could you please tell me who handles operations / clinic management, or what email is best for [Name/role]?
```

If they ask for detail:

```text
It is a small booking workflow: web form / phone / email / WhatsApp -> CRM -> instant patient reply -> staff notification -> appointment reminder -> missed inquiry follow-up. I’d like to send a 2-minute example, not a generic sales deck.
```
